Welcome to Neuralonic! With this web application, you can quickly check in on your emotions and mental state. Here’s a step-by-step guide:
01
Upload a Pre-recorded Voice File

  • Click on the "Upload" button.
  • Select an audio file (e.g., .wav or .mp3 format) from your device.
  • Wait for the file to upload and process. The website will display the predicted emotion.

    • 02
    Record Live Speech

  • Click on the "Record" button to start recording.
  • Speak clearly into your device’s microphone.
  • When you finish speaking, click on the "Stop" button.
  • The recording will automatically be processed, and the predicted emotion will be displayed.

    • 03
    Understanding the Results

    The website will display a predicted emotion (e.g., Happy, Sad, Angry) based on your voice input.

    04
    Troubleshooting

  • If your microphone isn’t working, make sure it’s enabled in your device settings.
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection to process the audio files.
